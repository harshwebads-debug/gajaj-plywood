Quality. Service. Innovation.

Wood panels & wood wall paneling have been done traditionally using solid wood timber but with the gradual time lapse and rising matters pertaining to solid wood availability, plywood panels have become the most preferred product nowadays. In a vast country like India where we have multiple climatic zones & environmental factors affecting product performance every day (humidity, moisture, heat, dust), wooden wall panels or decorative wooden panels need to be carefully crafted as well as curated to withstand these elements.

Not every other wood panel product available in the market complies with such specific needs or undergoes rigorous testing from time to time. Gajraj Plywood as a responsible brand understands all these critical factors and accordingly has developed customer-centric products. Right from the careful selection of raw materials to individual veneer seasoning, Gajraj makes sure that only full core & full panel veneers are used for all plywood products. Similarly, all pinewood & hardwood timber for Block Boards as well as Flush Doors construction are carefully ripped and thereafter seasoned (kiln seasoning + air seasoning) for providing the best quality products.

The entire gamut of products comprising of Block Board, Waterproof Plywood, Hardwood Plywood, Moisture Resistant Plywood, and Flush Doors are all made to deliver performance beyond expectations and comes with warranty coverage. Therefore, with a customer-centric approach along with a best-in-class manufacturing process, every Gajraj Plywood product has some unique features which make them stand out in the market and rightly caters to the need of our Indian consumers.
